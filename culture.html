Kazakh Culture Highlights

Traditional Clothing

Kazakh people wear richly embroidered garments made from silk, wool, and leather, reflecting centuries of nomadic heritage and Islamic influences.

Know More

Yurt Living

The yurt, or "kiyiz ui", is a traditional portable home used by nomads. It symbolizes hospitality and deep respect for nature in Kazakh culture.

Kazakh Cuisine

From hearty dishes like *beshbarmak* to fermented mare’s milk known as *kumys*, Kazakh cuisine offers a unique blend of flavors and history.
